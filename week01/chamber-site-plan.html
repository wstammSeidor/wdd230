Site Plan

Site name: Chamber Of Commerce

Purpose

The purpose of the site is to highlight all the benefits for those who are part of the chamber, and to make it easy for users to request more information or get in touch so they can also join.

Image carousel with success case descriptions and Color Schema

The color palette would be in a range of blue tones, as it tends to provide a more professional appearance.

Typography

The typography would be Roboto, as it is a very legible font and it is also very versatile.

Chamber of Commerce Site Plan Scenarios

1. Member Registration:

A new local business interested in joining the Chamber of Commerce. Describe the registration process and membership benefits.

2. Business Search:

Entrepreneur looking for local suppliers. Explain how to use the website to find member businesses.

3. Events and Activities:

Member interested in participating in events. Detail how to access the events calendar and register.

4. Business Directory:

Tourist seeking information about local businesses. Show how to use the directory on the website.

Navigation

Within the nav, there would be links to both the list of chamber members and also a form to get in touch with the organization.

In the footer, I will include all the social networks of the chamber.
